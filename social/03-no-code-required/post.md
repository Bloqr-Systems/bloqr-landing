# No Code Required. Ever.

The internet hygiene that protects privacy experts looks intimidating:

- Self-hosted AdGuard Home cluster
- Custom YAML configs
- GitHub-hosted filter lists
- Cron jobs to sync updates
- Red Hat servers on a home rack

That's how Bloqr's founder used to do it — and it took years to get right.

**You should never have to.**

Bloqr AI is built for the person who:
- Has an ad blocker installed but couldn't explain how it works
- Wants their family protected on every device, without a home lab
- Sees the word "configuration" and closes the tab

Here's what your experience looks like:

1. Create an account
2. Pick a DNS provider from our curated list (or bring your own)
3. Bloqr AI builds your list, activates your protection, keeps everything current

That's it. One switch. You're done.

The code exists. The servers exist. The AI is running.
*You'll never see any of it.*
