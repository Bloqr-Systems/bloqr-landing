THREAD: WHAT IS DNS FILTERING?

Your browser encrypts what you read on a page. Great.

But your device reaches out to trackers, ad networks, and bad actors *before* your browser even loads the page.

DNS filtering blocks those requests at the source — before they leave your device.

No traffic routed through servers. No VPN. Just local blocking.
